Our Infrastructure

Our School Infrastructure is a part of our most valued objective viz. world-class teaching. State - of - Art infrastructure assists each student in their holistic grooming. Computer lab, Laboratories, Mathematics labs, Sports Academy, Digital Library and Smart Classrooms - which are furnished with ultra-modern, teaching methodologies that can compete with world standards.

Our Research and Development Units

A. ADVANCED ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE (AI) LAB

Gurukulam Global School boasts of an advanced Computer and AI Lab where the students are made FUTURE READY by the experts. Computers to Students Ratio is the best in the industry offering adequate hands-on practice for our students. Course curriculum includes all the latest computer applications matching pace with the technological requirements.

B. LANGUAGE LAB:

Gurukulam Global School strongly believes that each child is unique and everyone should learn HOW TO EXPRESS. Communication is one of the most important qualities in today's world. Our dedication and world class language lab is fully equipped with training facilities that help each student to learn the indepth of communication, effectively. The lab is equipped to train different languages.

C. ROBOTICS LAB

Robotic is one of the promising sectors for tomorrow. Realizing its importance the school has set up a DEDICATED ROBOTICS LAB to train the engineers of the future.

D. DIGITAL LIBRARY

Gurukulam Global School trusts the ideology of ‘Today’s Reader, Tomorrow’s Leader’. Our library is a trove of most advanced and academic books to help students to cultivate the habit of reading. One can find the best and most useful books of every subject, in our library. Interactive UI and attractive displays of our digital library offers periodicals, magazines, newspapers etc. besides the story and academic book treasure.

SMART CLASSES

Gurukualm Global School is equipped with the most advanced interactive smart classrooms which takes learning to the next level. Our students not only find it easy to understand the toughest concepts using these tools but it also makes LEARNING A FUN.

Air Conditioned and Sound Proof Classrooms and Transport

"A healthy mind resides in a healthy body". Going by the proverb, we have focused on physical activities for the OVERALL GROWTH of our students. Sports is a major part of Gurukulam Global School's curriculum because if students stay emotionally and physically healthy, they can easily focus on their studies. Students have to participate in school sports to increase confidence, mental alertness, and self-esteem.
