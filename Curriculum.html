Beyond Books Curriculum

At Gurukulam Global School curriculum is tailored with multidimensional child centric approach, which is both ineractive and practical. Our curriculum, both in terms of content and methodology, is 21st century relevant and meets with the upcoming standards.Comprising a dynamic and colorfull porpourri of academics in addition to art integrated learning, sports, artificial intelligence and personality development aimed at creativity and exposing kids to varied educational preparing them to face the future challanges of globalised economy, We nurture children to be confident, self-sufficient, havind independent thought process and never ending urge for learning.

Personality Grooming and Communication

Gurukulam Global School is working dedicatedly on PERSONALITY GROOMING and COMMUNICATION SKILLS of our students. Personality development helps students to develop the communication skills they need to be successful in their studies. Leadership is an important quality for success in any field. Students who want to achieve success in their chosen career path need to be able to lead others.

=

Sports Academy

"A healthy mind resides in a healthy body". Going by the proverb, we have focused on physical activities for the OVERALL GROWTH of our students. Sports is a major part of Gurukulam Global School's curriculum because if students stay emotionally and physically healthy, they can easily focus on their studies. Students have to participate in school sports to increase confidence, mental alertness, and self-esteem.

Art Integrated Learning

At Gurukulam Global School, we use Art Integrated Learning (AIL) which is a teaching-learning model based on learning 'THROUGH THE ARTS' AND 'WITH THE ARTS'. Through this process, we make art and creativity as the medium of teaching-learning which enhances understanding concepts within any subject of the curriculum

Artificial Intelligence and Robotics

Gurukulam Global School is dedicated to match the pace with the changing times. With the rapid development of artificial intelligence, the application of AI robots (Artificial Intelligence-based robots) for instruction has become an ATTRACTIVE RESEARCH TOPIC. Numerous studies have shown that AI robots may provide new opportunities for learning designs in school settings or professional training, which has been inducted efficiently in GGS.
