From the Desk of Chairman

The winds of globalisation and socio st technological advancement of the 21st century is day by day providing threat to mankind by curtailing and amending the values and virtues that past Gurus have imbibed in us. The aggressive rush for materialistic self - fulfilment and competitiveness is redefining our socio cultural ethics and undermining the true self of all beings. Hence, at this critical juncture the need has been arisen to negate the undesirable impacts of these changes and imbibe the desirable and expected virtues by providing the life oriented skills and value oriented atmosphere. Gurukulam Global School believes in rendering such education that will bring out the true Indian. Gurukulam Global School will train the young generation to view life holistically, creatively and philosophically, face challenges in life with a positive attitude, develop skills in responding emergencies, promote social and environmental awareness and live a better and a meaningful life through self actualization.The teaching methodologies at GGS are equipped with life skills that will enable them to resist materialism, manage imbalance of life successfully and develop appreciation for nature both external and internal. Then and then only it is possible to expose our children to wisdom and creativity so that they sustain our ancient culture. To conclude, all I would like to say is that your child was a gift from God to you now let us together make the child worthy and this will be our best gift to God. Our former President APJ Abdul Kalam asked, "Can we make an education system which will retain the smiles on the faces of our children throughout their education?" We are committed to meet this challenge. GGS is taking the lead in implementing NEP2020, talent management, sealing technology and promoting student agency. I hope the school fulfils its vision in every sense.
-Mr. Sanjeev Sharma
(Chairman)

From the Desk of Advsior

We place a high premium on the role of teachers, their competencies, skills and ability to nurture talent. Rapid changes in technology and globalisation are drastically changing the education scenario. This change will require a sense of urgency in creating a new collaborative and interdisciplinary approach to enrich our teaching-learning process. We need to prepare the students for the modern world and who can become responsible and engaged members of society, resulting in sustainable growth, peace and positive global impact. School’s motto "Acieving Excellence together" is a driving force to pitch students, their thoughts and creativity with their counterparts in the different time zones of the world. They are encouraged to form online learning communities, collaborate on joint projects, exchange visits and learn the value of appreciation for different cultures. Preparing children for life calls for equipping them with exceptional values and academic competencies. Our alumni have found places in the top universities in India and abroad, covering a broad range of disciplines. Most of them now work with prestigious organisations all over the world. Their success stories validate and fuel our efforts.
-Mr. M M Sharma
(Advisor)
